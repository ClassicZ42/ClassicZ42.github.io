Jacquard Interiors has been operating since 1998 and have completed many projects big and small for domestic and corporate clients. We don’t work with a specific style but rather to our client’s specific style and taste. We work with our clients to accomplish their desired end result. We pride ourselves in our quality and workmanship. Decorating should be an exciting and rewarding experience, however heading into a project without doing your research can be a disastrous and costly affair. It is important not to make random purchases or decisions. A successful decorating project should involve research, thought, planning and consultation. Don’t be afraid to seek professional advice. Good advice is invaluable and more often than not more cost effective.
